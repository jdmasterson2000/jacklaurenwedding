Travel and Lodging

The closest airport is the Cincinnati Airport. Public transportation is sadly not as great as it for our East Coast friends, so we recommend you take an Uber from the airport to your hotel.

The next closest airport is the Dayton airport in Lauren's hometown. However, it's about an hour drive from there to downtown Cincy so we only recommend this option if you have a rental car or some sort of ride to Cincinnati.

Hotel Recommendations

For convenience, we of course recommend The Lytle Park Hotel. However, we recognize that this may not be feasible due to price or amenity preference. Luckily Cincy has a number of hotels close to the wedding!

The next best hotel would be Residence Inn Marriott Cincinnati Downtown/The Phelps which is right across the street from Lytle Park.

Here are some other options to consider:

  • Renaissance Cincinnati Downtown Hotel
  • The Westin Cincinnati
  • Holiday Inn and Suites Cincinnati Downtown (right by St. Xavier Church)
