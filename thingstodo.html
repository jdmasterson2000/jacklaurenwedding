Attractions

  • Cincinnati Zoo: One of the best zoos in the country. They have a wonderful collection of felines and a delightful manatee exhibit.
  • Findlay Market: Ohio's oldest continuously operated public market. Located in the Over-the-Rhine district and is the source of farm fresh, locally sourced, artisanal, and specialty foods.
  • Ault Park: A beautiful regional park with lookout points to view the majestic Ohio River.
  • Cincinnati Art Museum: Contains one of the most comprehensive collections in the midwest. It spans over 6000 years with over 67,000 works.
  • Newport Aquarium: Known for their first-of-its-kind shark ray breeding program, the Newport Aquarium has two albino alligators, five acrylic tunnels, and 3.8 million liters of water.

Bars

  • Ghost Baby: You can reserve a table in advance or try your luck at this notorious speakeasy.
  • Rosedale: Located in the Over the Rhine (OTR) district, described as "a lovely bar."
  • Somerset: Cool, stylish, incredible drinks. The place to be!

Nice Restaurants

  • Sotto: The most sought after Italian food in the city. Incredible food, atmosphere, and wine. Later reservations are usually open, but you need to book ahead.
  • Nada: Great upscale Mexican food.
  • Pepp and Dolores: Very popular pasta joint. Reserve ahead.

Casual Restaurants

  • Fireside Pizza: Great pizza in an old firehouse building. Amazing staff and family friendly. A place that everyone will like!
  • SKYLINE: You have to.
  • Parkside: Located in Hyde Park Square, best burger in the city. Very chill and family friendly.
  • The Bagelry: A Cincinnati classic! Order ahead on the app.
  • Maplewood Kitchen: Amazing brunch. Get there early.
  • Bakersfield: Located in the heart of OTR, great tacos!
  • Che: Located in both OTR and Hyde Park, this upscale empanada joint will not disappoint. More pricey, but worth it.
  • Incline Public House: Classic American food with amazing views of the city! If you've never been to Cincinnati this is a cool place to go.
  • The Eagle: The city's best and most popular chicken restaurant located in OTR. It is very popular! Hop across the street after for some Graeter's Ice Cream (Lauren's all time favorite)

Coffee Shops

  • Deeper Roots: Great to explore the Oakley area.
  • Awakenings: Located in Hyde Park Square, situated in the heart of the East Side.
